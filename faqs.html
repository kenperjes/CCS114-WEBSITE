Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about the University of Cabuyao? Find answers to the most commonly asked inquiries about admissions, academic programs, student services, campus facilities, and more. Whether you're a prospective student, a parent, or a member of the UCab community, this page provides the essential information you need. Explore our FAQs for quick and convenient answers.

To apply for admission, the university typically announces when it will start. To stay updated, follow the university's social media page.

The office hours of the university start at 7:00 AM and close at 5:00 PM.

The facilities that are currently available at the university are a skills lab, library, laboratory, and computer laboratory.

To contact the university, you can go to the University of Cabuyao Facebook page for any inquiries.

There's no tuition fee at the University of Cabuyao because of RA 10931, also known as UNIFAST.
