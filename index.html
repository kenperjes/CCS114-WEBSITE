ABOUT US

Get to know more about our university

The University of Cabuyao is built on a strong foundation of excellence, integrity, and service. Guided by our mission, vision, and core values, we are committed to shaping future leaders through quality education, innovation, and community engagement. These guiding principles define who we are, what we strive for, and how we prepare our students to make a meaningful impact in society.

Our Vision

A premier institution of higher learning in Region IV, developing globally competitive and value laden professionals and leaders intrumental to community development and nation building.

Our Mission

As an institution of higher learning, PnC is committed to equip individuals with knowledge, skills, and values that will enable them to achieve their professional goals & provide leadership and service for national development.

Core Values

As a God-fearing institution, respecting multi faith of people, PnC adheres to the following core values:
P - Personal Dignity
N - Nurturing Community
C - Commitment to Excellence

Quality Policy

Pamantasan ng Cabuyao commits itself in producing quality graduates by adhering to statutory and regulatory requirements, promoting high levels of customer engagement, and maintaining an effective quality management system through periodic review and communication of quality objectives for continous improvement of quality services in instruction, research, and extension.

Graduate Attributes

1.Professional Competence 2.Communication Skills
3.Analytical and Problem-Solving Skills 4.Lifelong Learning Skills
5.Interpersonal Skills
6.Productivity
7.Social and Ethical Responsibility



Quality Objectives

1. To promote analytical thinking among the faculty and students for continuing intellectual growth and advancement of learning and research. 2.To develop the youth to become responsible leaders, as well as productive and actively involved citizens of the local and global community with good values and excellent character.


PROGRAMS OFFERED

Here are the list of programs for you

The University of Cabuyao offers a diverse range of programs designed to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and expertise needed for success in their chosen fields.

Bachelor of Science in Information Technology

Develop technical expertise in software development, networking, and cybersecurity to thrive in the ever-evolving world of technology.

Bachelor of Science in Accountancy

Build a strong foundation in financial management, auditing, and taxation to become a competent and ethical accounting professional.

Bachelor of Science in Industrial Engineering

Learn to optimize systems, processes, and resources to enhance efficiency and productivity across industries.

Bachelor of Science in Nursing

Gain the knowledge, skills, and compassion needed to provide quality healthcare and make a difference in patients' lives.

Bachelor of Science in Education

Prepare to inspire and shape future generations with effective teaching strategies and a deep understanding of education principles.

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

Master the fundamentals of computing, programming, and artificial intelligence to innovate and solve complex technological challenges.

Bachelor of Science in Psychology

Explore human behavior, mental processes, and psychological research to promote well-being and drive positive change in society.
